Manifesto of the Peaceful Occupation

At the time of writing, the State of Israel is committing ethnic cleansing. Since the beginning of its siege, the Gaza Strip has been cut off from deliveries of food, water, fuel, medicine and humanitarian aid. As a result of bombing and shelling, hospitals, universities, schools and other civilian infrastructure in which the Palestinian population could seeks shelter are being continuously razed. As a result of the Israeli government's unimaginable violence and cruelty, the death toll, as well as the number of displaced and evicted Palestinians is increasing every day. Faced with these events, we believe that it is our ethical and moral responsibility to stand in categorical, resolute and conscious solidarity with the Palestinian people, and that this warrants specific action. A week has passed since the rector of the UWr was handed our open letter. Having received no response other than the empty words of the statement of the Conference of Rectors of Polish Universities, we declare the start of a peaceful, occupational strike. Seeing no other way forward, in solidarity with students in Poland and abroad (University of Warsaw and Jagiellonian University), we have decided to occupy the public space of the University. As an institution, it should represent the voice of the academic community, and not just it’s authorities. In light of the above, we call on the University of Wrocław to:
1. publicly, firmly and unequivocally condemn Israel's attack on the Gaza Strip and it’s occupation of Palestine,
2. immediately cease cooperation with Israeli academic institutions, research centers and other organizations and companies,
3. release all information concerning UWr’s cooperation with such institutions,
4. boycott Israeli institutions at the national and international level until the occupation of Palestine ends, recognize the right of Palestinians to equality and self-determination, and recognize the right of return for Palestinian refugees.
With the ever-present issue of anti-Semitism in mind, we stress that the fundamental value motivating our actions is an opposition to the oppression of any ethnic or racial minority. There is no consent on our part for any manifestation of racism, Islamophobia, anti-Semitism and Arabophobia. Never again for anyone.

27.05.2024

We, the students of the University of Wrocław, devastated and horrified by Israel's criminal actions against the Palestinian people, believe that it is time to stop waiting and watching the situation with fear and start taking action. For this reason, we have decided to draft an open letter to our university, in which we call on the Uniwersity of Wrocław to:
1. publicly, firmly and unequivocally condemn Israel's attack on the Gaza Strip and occupation of Palestine,
2. immediately cease cooperation with Israeli academic institutions, research centers and other organizations and companies,
3. make information available about which such entities the UWr is cooperating with and to what extent,
4. boycott Israeli institutions at the national and international level until the occupation of Palestine ends, recognize the right of Palestinians to equality and self-determination, and recognise the right of return for Palestinian refugees.
We would also like to ask everyone to support us in this action. Anyone, not just students, can sign the open letter and share information about our activities. We do not intend to stop at drafting and delivering this message to the authorities of UWr. Therefore, if you want to support us, you are warmly welcome!
